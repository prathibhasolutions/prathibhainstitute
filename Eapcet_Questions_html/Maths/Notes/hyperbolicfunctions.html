Hyperbolic Functions

Definition of Hyperbolic Functions

Function Expression
\( \sinh x \) \( \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{2} \)
\( \cosh x \) \( \frac{e^x + e^{-x}}{2} \)
\( \tanh x \) \( \frac{\sinh x}{\cosh x} = \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{e^x + e^{-x}} \)
\( \coth x \) \( \frac{\cosh x}{\sinh x} = \frac{e^x + e^{-x}}{e^x - e^{-x}} \)
\( sech x \) \( \frac{1}{\cosh x} = \frac{2}{e^x + e^{-x}} \)
\( csch x \) \( \frac{1}{\sinh x} = \frac{2}{e^x - e^{-x}} \)

Domain and Range of Hyperbolic Functions

Domain and Range

Function Domain Range
\( \sinh x \) \( (-\infty, \infty) \) \( (-\infty, \infty) \)
\( \cosh x \) \( (-\infty, \infty) \) \( [1, \infty) \)
\( \tanh x \) \( (-\infty, \infty) \) \( (-1, 1) \)
\( \coth x \) \( (-\infty, \infty) \setminus \{0\} \) \( (-\infty, -1) \cup (1, \infty) \)
\( sech x \) \( (-\infty, \infty) \) \( (0, 1] \)
\( csch x \) \( (-\infty, \infty) \setminus \{0\} \) \( (-\infty, 0) \cup (0, \infty) \)

Hyperbolic Identities

Fundamental Identities
  • \( \cosh^2 x - \sinh^2 x = 1 \)
  • \( 1 - \tanh^2 x = sech^2 x \)
  • \( \coth^2 x - 1 = csch^2 x \)
Addition and Subtraction Formulas
  • \( \sinh (a + b) = \sinh a \cosh b + \cosh a \sinh b \)
  • \( \sinh (a - b) = \sinh a \cosh b - \cosh a \sinh b \)
  • \( \cosh (a + b) = \cosh a \cosh b + \sinh a \sinh b \)
  • \( \cosh (a - b) = \cosh a \cosh b - \sinh a \sinh b \)
  • \( \tanh (a + b) = \frac{\tanh a + \tanh b}{1 + \tanh a \tanh b} \)
  • \( \tanh (a - b) = \frac{\tanh a - \tanh b}{1 - \tanh a \tanh b} \)
Hyperbolic Function Relations
  • \( \tanh x = \frac{\sinh x}{\cosh x} \)
  • \( \coth x = \frac{\cosh x}{\sinh x} \)
  • \( sech x = \frac{1}{\cosh x} \)
  • \( csch x = \frac{1}{\sinh x} \)
Product-to-Sum Formulas
  • \( \sinh a \sinh b = \frac{1}{2} [\cosh (a - b) - \cosh (a + b)] \)
  • \( \cosh a \cosh b = \frac{1}{2} [\cosh (a + b) + \cosh (a - b)] \)
  • \( \sinh a \cosh b = \frac{1}{2} [\sinh (a + b) + \sinh (a - b)] \)
  • \( \cosh a \sinh b = \frac{1}{2} [\sinh (a + b) - \sinh (a - b)] \)
Sum-to-Product Formulas
  • \( \sinh a + \sinh b = 2 \sinh \frac{(a+b)}{2} \cosh \frac{(a-b)}{2} \)
  • \( \sinh a - \sinh b = 2 \cosh \frac{(a+b)}{2} \sinh \frac{(a-b)}{2} \)
  • \( \cosh a + \cosh b = 2 \cosh \frac{(a+b)}{2} \cosh \frac{(a-b)}{2} \)
  • \( \cosh a - \cosh b = 2 \sinh \frac{(a+b)}{2} \sinh \frac{(a-b)}{2} \)

Hyperbolic Multiple Angle Formulas

Double Angle Formulas
  • \( \sinh 2x = 2 \sinh x \cosh x \)
  • \( \cosh 2x = \cosh^2 x + \sinh^2 x = 2\cosh^2 x - 1 = 1 + 2\sinh^2 x \)
  • \( \tanh 2x = \frac{2 \tanh x}{1 + \tanh^2 x} \)
  • \( \coth 2x = \frac{\coth^2 x + 1}{2 \coth x} \)
Triple Angle Formulas
  • \( \sinh 3x = 3\sinh x + 4\sinh^3 x \)
  • \( \cosh 3x = 4\cosh^3 x - 3\cosh x \)
  • \( \tanh 3x = \frac{3\tanh x + \tanh^3 x}{1 + 3\tanh^2 x} \)

Domain & Range of Inverse Hyperbolic Functions

Function Domain Range
\(\sinh^{-1} x\) \(\mathbb{R}\) (All real numbers) \(\mathbb{R}\)
\(\cosh^{-1} x\) \([1, \infty)\) \([0, \infty)\)
\(\tanh^{-1} x\) \((-1,1)\) \(\mathbb{R}\)
\(\coth^{-1} x\) \((-\infty, -1) \cup (1, \infty)\) \(\mathbb{R}\)
\(sech^{-1} x\) \((0,1]\) \([0, \infty)\)
\(csch^{-1} x\) \(\mathbb{R} \setminus \{0\}\) \(\mathbb{R} \setminus \{0\}\)
Inverse Hyperbolic Functions in Logarithmic Form
  • \(\sinh^{-1} x = \ln \left( x + \sqrt{x^2 + 1} \right) \)
  • \(\cosh^{-1} x = \ln \left( x + \sqrt{x^2 - 1} \right) \),   \( x \geq 1 \)
  • \(\tanh^{-1} x = \frac{1}{2} \ln \left( \frac{1 + x}{1 - x} \right) \),   \( |x| < 1 \)
  • \(\coth^{-1} x = \frac{1}{2} \ln \left( \frac{x + 1}{x - 1} \right) \),   \( |x| > 1 \)
  • \(sech^{-1} x = \ln \left( \frac{1 + \sqrt{1 - x^2}}{x} \right) \),   \( 0 < x \leq 1 \)
  • \(csch^{-1} x = \ln \left( \frac{1}{x} + \frac{\sqrt{1 + x^2}}{|x|} \right) \),   \( x \neq 0 \)
