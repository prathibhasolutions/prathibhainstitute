Inverse Trigonometric Functions

Domain & Principal Value (Range) of Inverse Trigonometric Functions
Function Domain Principal Value (Range)
\(\sin^{-1} x\) \([-1, 1]\) \(\left[-\frac{\pi}{2}, \frac{\pi}{2}\right]\)
\(\cos^{-1} x\) \([-1, 1]\) \([0, \pi]\)
\(\tan^{-1} x\) \((-\infty, \infty)\) \(\left(-\frac{\pi}{2}, \frac{\pi}{2}\right)\)
\(\cot^{-1} x\) \((-\infty, \infty)\) \((0, \pi)\)
\(\sec^{-1} x\) \((-\infty, -1] \cup [1, \infty)\) \([0, \pi] \setminus \{\frac{\pi}{2}\}\)
\(\csc^{-1} x\) \((-\infty, -1] \cup [1, \infty)\) \(\left[-\frac{\pi}{2}, \frac{\pi}{2} \right] \setminus \{0\}\)
Elementary Properties of Inverse Trigonometric Functions
  • \(\sin^{-1}(\sin \theta) = \theta\), if \(\theta \in \left[-\frac{\pi}{2}, \frac{\pi}{2}\right]\)
  • \(\cos^{-1}(\cos \theta) = \theta\), if \(\theta \in [0, \pi]\)
  • \(\tan^{-1}(\tan \theta) = \theta\), if \(\theta \in \left(-\frac{\pi}{2}, \frac{\pi}{2}\right)\)
  • \(\cot^{-1}(\cot \theta) = \theta\), if \(\theta \in (0, \pi)\)
  • \(\sec^{-1}(\sec \theta) = \theta\), if \(\theta \in [0, \pi] \setminus \{\frac{\pi}{2}\}\)
  • \(\csc^{-1}(\csc \theta) = \theta\), if \(\theta \in \left[-\frac{\pi}{2}, \frac{\pi}{2}\right] \setminus \{0\}\)
Elementary Properties of Inverse Trigonometric Functions
  • \(\sin(\sin^{-1} x) = x\), for \( x \in [-1,1] \)
  • \(\cos(\cos^{-1} x) = x\), for \( x \in [-1,1] \)
  • \(\tan(\tan^{-1} x) = x\), for \( x \in (-\infty, \infty) \)
  • \(\cot(\cot^{-1} x) = x\), for \( x \in (-\infty, \infty) \)
  • \(\sec(\sec^{-1} x) = x\), for \( x \in (-\infty, -1] \cup [1, \infty) \)
  • \(\csc(\csc^{-1} x) = x\), for \( x \in (-\infty, -1] \cup [1, \infty) \)
Elementary Properties of Inverse Trigonometric Functions
  • \(\sin^{-1}(-x) = -\sin^{-1}x\), for \( x \in [-1,1] \)
  • \(\cos^{-1}(-x) = \pi - \cos^{-1}x\), for \( x \in [-1,1] \)
  • \(\tan^{-1}(-x) = -\tan^{-1}x\), for \( x \in (-\infty, \infty) \)
  • \(\cot^{-1}(-x) = \pi - \cot^{-1}x\), for \( x \in (-\infty, \infty) \)
  • \(\sec^{-1}(-x) = \pi - \sec^{-1}x\), for \( x \in (-\infty, -1] \cup [1, \infty) \)
  • \(\csc^{-1}(-x) = -\csc^{-1}x\), for \( x \in (-\infty, -1] \cup [1, \infty) \)
Elementary Properties of \( \sin^{-1}(1/x) \) and Similar Functions
  • \(\sin^{-1}(1/x) = \csc^{-1}(x)\), for \( |x| \geq 1 \)
  • \(\cos^{-1}(1/x) = \sec^{-1}(x)\), for \( |x| \geq 1 \)
  • \(\tan^{-1}(1/x) = \frac{\pi}{2} - \tan^{-1}x\), for \( x > 0 \)
  • \(\tan^{-1}(1/x) = -\frac{\pi}{2} - \tan^{-1}x\), for \( x < 0 \)
  • \(\cot^{-1}(1/x) = \frac{\pi}{2} - \cot^{-1}x\), for \( x > 0 \)
  • \(\cot^{-1}(1/x) = \frac{3\pi}{2} - \cot^{-1}x\), for \( x < 0 \)
  • \(\sec^{-1}(1/x) = \cos^{-1}(x)\), for \( |x| \geq 1 \)
  • \(\csc^{-1}(1/x) = \sin^{-1}(x)\), for \( |x| \geq 1 \)
Conversion Between Inverse Trigonometric Functions
  • \(\sin^{-1}x = \cos^{-1} \sqrt{1 - x^2}\), for \( x \in [-1,1] \).
  • \(\cos^{-1}x = \sin^{-1} \sqrt{1 - x^2}\), for \( x \in [-1,1] \).
  • \(\tan^{-1}x = \cot^{-1} \frac{1}{x}\), for \( x > 0 \).
  • \(\cot^{-1}x = \tan^{-1} \frac{1}{x}\), for \( x > 0 \).
  • \(\sec^{-1}x = \csc^{-1} \frac{1}{x}\), for \( |x| \geq 1 \).
  • \(\csc^{-1}x = \sec^{-1} \frac{1}{x}\), for \( |x| \geq 1 \).
Sum of Inverse Trigonometric Functions
  • \(\sin^{-1}x + \cos^{-1}x = \frac{\pi}{2}\), for \( x \in [-1,1] \)
  • \(\tan^{-1}x + \cot^{-1}x = \frac{\pi}{2}\), for \( x > 0 \)
  • \(\sec^{-1}x + \csc^{-1}x = \frac{\pi}{2}\), for \( |x| \geq 1 \)
Sum and Difference of Inverse Trigonometric Functions
  • \(\sin^{-1}x + \sin^{-1}y = \sin^{-1} \left( x \sqrt{1 - y^2} + y \sqrt{1 - x^2} \right)\), for \( x, y \in [-1,1] \) and \( x^2 + y^2 \leq 1 \).
  • \(\sin^{-1}x - \sin^{-1}y = \sin^{-1} \left( x \sqrt{1 - y^2} - y \sqrt{1 - x^2} \right)\), for \( x, y \in [-1,1] \) and \( x^2 + y^2 \leq 1 \).
  • \(\cos^{-1}x + \cos^{-1}y = \cos^{-1} \left( xy - \sqrt{(1 - x^2)(1 - y^2)} \right)\), for \( x, y \in [-1,1] \) and \( x^2 + y^2 \leq 1 \).
  • \(\cos^{-1}x - \cos^{-1}y = \cos^{-1} \left( xy + \sqrt{(1 - x^2)(1 - y^2)} \right)\), for \( x, y \in [-1,1] \) and \( x^2 + y^2 \leq 1 \).
  • \(\tan^{-1}x + \tan^{-1}y = \tan^{-1} \left( \frac{x + y}{1 - xy} \right)\), for \( xy < 1 \).
  • \(\tan^{-1}x - \tan^{-1}y = \tan^{-1} \left( \frac{x - y}{1 + xy} \right)\), for all \( x, y \).
Double Angle of Inverse Trigonometric Functions
  • \( 2\sin^{-1}x = \sin^{-1} \left( 2x \sqrt{1 - x^2} \right) \), for \( x \in [-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}, \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}] \).
  • \( 2\cos^{-1}x = \cos^{-1} \left( 2x^2 - 1 \right) \), for \( x \in [0,1] \).
  • \( 2\tan^{-1}x = \tan^{-1} \left( \frac{2x}{1 - x^2} \right) \), for \( |x| < 1 \).
Triple Angle of Inverse Trigonometric Functions
  • \( 3\sin^{-1}x = \sin^{-1} \left( 3x - 4x^3 \right) \), for \( x \in [-\frac{1}{2}, \frac{1}{2}] \).
  • \( 3\cos^{-1}x = \cos^{-1} \left( 4x^3 - 3x \right) \), for \( x \in [0,1] \).
  • \( 3\tan^{-1}x = \tan^{-1} \left( \frac{3x - x^3}{1 - 3x^2} \right) \), for \( |x| < \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \).
